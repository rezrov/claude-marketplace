# My Interests

Paperboy reads this file at every run and uses it as the spec for its classifier.
Edit freely — add, remove, or rephrase rules. Marginal items are rejected by default.

## Keep

- **Technology news**, with particular interest in articles that involve new developments, tools, and capabilities surrounding artificial intelligence.
- **USA News**, with a focus on major events that have a direct impact on a relatively large number of American citizens — such as sudden economic changes, weather or natural disasters, or serious events in American politics like major resignations, assassinations, major court rulings, and the outcomes of major elections.
- **World News** that involves major events that have a direct impact on a relatively large number of people — major economic changes, natural disasters, and major events involving military conflicts either starting, significantly escalating, or significantly de-escalating.

## Strongly reject (in all cases)

- Opinion articles and editorials.
- "Clickbait".
- News surrounding the ever-present turmoil in American and world politics that does not have an immediate impact on citizens.
- Highly sensationalized topics.
- "Doomscrolling" type articles.
